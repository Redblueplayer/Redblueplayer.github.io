Arcane Odyssey:

Critical Strike:
A really fun class-based fighting game featuring a very fast-paced and pleasant gameplay, and having over 50 classes.
Try it here

Defend the train:
A round-based wave defense where you have to defend a train (duh) from hordes of enemies who will try to stop you from getting to the next station.
Play here

Starscape:
A space themed MMORPG where you are a space mercenary doing missions for the different NPC factions in the game.
